Wealth Generation Lets make some Money.

WEALTH GENERATION is a Peer to Peer mutual investment system where where members get rewarded from helping each others. This is the next big thing to liberate your financial burden.

Get Started

How to use

In 3 easy steps, you can conveniently get that donation you really need.

1

Registration: During registration, make sure you use your correct mobile number. This is how other members can communicate with you when you are getting paid. After registration, Proceed to request for shares

2

Login: After your registration fee is confirmed, you will be able to login to your account, otherwise you will be stuck on the registration payment page. To login your account, you will need your username and password. Kindly chat with online support or group admins when you lose or forget Your password.

3

Invest: When you login to your account after registration, on your dashboard click on invest. On the box provided, input your desired investment amount. Note that minimum amount to invest is 500ksh and the maximum amount is 5000ksh.

Key Features

Timer: Immediately you are comfirmed, your timer for 2 days will start counting down. During these period, you will be allocated a receiver.

Make Payment: After your investment is allocated to a receiver, all you need to do is to login to your account, make payment and upload your proof of payment. You are also required (or not) to call the recipient for quick confirmation of your payment. Note that you will be accorded 3 hours to Make payment. Otherwise, your account will be blocked and pending investment(s) will be cancelled.

Get Paid: When your timer is over, a cashout button will be visible, click on it, you will be allocated a payer with 30% interest. Make sure you confirm on the site when you receive your payment.
